What DiscoveryOS does

01  Extract

AI-powered extraction

Automatically reads feedback from files, calls, tickets, and surveys. Extracts structured pain points from unstructured text using Gemini.

02  Score

Transparent scoring

Every theme receives a priority score built from five weighted dimensions — customer impact, severity, business impact, strategic alignment, and segment value. No black boxes.

03  Prioritize

Strategy-adaptive prioritization

Switch between strategies — Improve Retention, Increase Revenue, Reduce Churn — and watch priorities re-rank instantly. The data stays the same; the lens changes.
